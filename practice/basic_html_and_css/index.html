Gino Antimarino chosen as OTB Anchor Fall 2024.

Max Gifford and co. chose Antimirano as the only junior anchor.

In the first Major League Baseball regular-season game played on South Korean soil, the Dodgers broke through with four runs in the top of the eighth on their way to a 5-2 victory.

Meanwhile, they literally broke through in another way: With the score tied 2-2, a routine Gavin Lux grounder punctured the webbing of Padres infielder Jake Cronenworth’s glove. Instead of winding up as double-play fodder, the ball squirted into right field. Teoscar Hernández scored the go-ahead run from second base.
